# GeoTaste AI Demo Script

## 15-Second Opening

GeoTaste AI helps F&B brands decide where to open and what to sell by analyzing local competitor reviews. Instead of guessing what Tampines or Jurong wants, the agent finds the complaints customers are already leaving nearby.

## 90-Second Walkthrough

1. Choose a brand profile, such as an Old Chang Kee-style snack chain.
2. Choose a target location, such as Tampines Mall / MRT.
3. Click `Live Sponsor Run`.
4. Show the competitor scan: nearby restaurants, ratings, distance, and review volume.
5. Show the complaint clusters: queues, cold food, poor takeaway, expensive portions, and late-day stock-outs.
6. Show the expansion brief: recommended concept, underserved craving, launch menu, pricing guardrail, and risk.
7. Point to the integration log: Bright Data scraping, Daytona sandbox execution, Kimi/TokenRouter reasoning, SenseNova reporting, Terminal 3 identity.

If live keys are not ready, use `Cached Demo` and say: "The cached data is the fallback layer. The live path is wired into the backend and lights up when the sponsor keys are present."

## Closing Line

This is not another food chatbot. GeoTaste AI is a site-selection and menu-positioning agent for F&B operators who need neighborhood-level demand intelligence before they sign a lease.

## Judge Q&A

**Why will people pay for this?**
F&B expansion mistakes are expensive. Even a small kiosk lease, renovation, staffing, and opening inventory can cost far more than a monthly market-intelligence tool.

**What makes it agentic?**
The system does not only answer questions. It collects local data, classifies complaints, identifies gaps, generates a recommendation, and can refresh the scan on a schedule.

**What is production-ready next?**
Connect Bright Data collectors to Google Maps, food directories, delivery menus, and social/video sources; run the jobs in Daytona; route analysis through Kimi or TokenRouter; generate SenseNova reports for franchise teams.
